Mattress Removal Services


If you are looking for a reliable and convenient mattress removal service in the greater Newnan, GA area, Southside Metro Hauling is here for you. We provide you with professional mattress and box spring removal services.

We work with both residential and commercial customers and can remove one or several mattresses at a time. We are conscientious of the environment and instead of throwing your mattress into a landfill, Southside Metro Hauling will make sure acceptable mattresses are recycled.

These types of mattresses include memory foam, innerspring, latex foam, and futon. In addition, box springs can be broken down and certain parts can also be recycled. When possible, can donate a mattress or box spring to charity.

We provide the best mattress removal deals and services to the greater Newnan, GA. Our mattress removal costs come with no hidden fees. When you call, you will receive an estimate free of charge with no obligation attached.
